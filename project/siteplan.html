Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about temples and heritage sites, including their locations, history, and cultural significance. The site will also provide tools that allow visitors to plan their trips and track travel-related expenses. The website will deliver interactive features, responsive design, and accessible content for users planning temple visits.

Scenarios

  • Which temples or heritage sites can I visit in a specific region?
  • How much should I budget for transportation, food, and accommodation when planning my temple visit?

Color Schema

Primary Color (#2E4057): Used for headers, navigation, and footer backgrounds.

Secondary Color (#F6AE2D): Used for highlights, buttons, and accent text.

Light gray (#F4F4F4) will be used as the main background color to improve readability.

Body Text: Roboto (Sans-serif)
